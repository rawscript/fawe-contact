Club Activities

From powerful performances to peer mentorship and national exposure, our club activities are designed to amplify student voices, build leadership, and drive meaningful change in school and beyond.

  • Democratic Elections: Members elect their leaders through guided, inclusive voting processes
  • Creative Advocacy: Use of drama, poetry, and spoken word to address issues like early pregnancy and school dropout
  • Parent Sensitization Forums: Performances and dialogue with families to promote education and student well-being
  • Career Exposure: Participation in university fairs and academic clinics to explore future pathways
  • Tuseme Days: Student-led forums to identify challenges and propose actionable solutions
  • Digital Campaigns: Plans to launch a YouTube channel and website to expand advocacy globally
